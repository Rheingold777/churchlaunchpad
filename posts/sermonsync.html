  • A central theme — the foundation for blog posts, devotionals, and more

Enter SermonSync

SermonSync is AI that actually understands ministry. Upload your sermon. Get a week of content. It's that simple.

How It Works

1. Upload Your Sermon

Audio, video, or even your notes. SermonSync transcribes and analyzes the entire message.

2. AI Extracts the Gold

The system identifies quotable moments, key points, scripture references, and emotional peaks. It understands sermon structure — intro, points, illustrations, altar call.

3. Get Ready-to-Post Content

Within minutes, you have:

  • 5-7 social media graphics with key quotes
  • Short video clips (30-60 seconds) of the best moments
  • Suggested captions for each platform
  • A weekly posting schedule
  • Optional: blog post draft expanding on the sermon theme

4. Review and Post

You approve everything before it goes live. Your voice, your message — just amplified.

One Sermon → 7 Days of Content

Here's what a typical week looks like with SermonSync:

  • Monday: Scripture graphic from Sunday's main text
  • Tuesday: Quote graphic from the sermon
  • Wednesday: 45-second video clip — powerful moment
  • Thursday: Story or illustration as a caption-only post
  • Friday: Another quote graphic
  • Saturday: Sermon teaser for Sunday
  • Sunday: Full sermon link + invite

That's a full content calendar from work you already did on Sunday.

FAQ

What format does my sermon need to be in?

Audio (MP3, WAV), video (MP4, MOV), or even your sermon notes/manuscript. We work with whatever you have.

How long does it take?

Processing takes about 10-15 minutes for a 30-minute sermon. You'll have your content ready within an hour of uploading.

Can I edit the content before it posts?

Absolutely. You review and approve everything. Nothing goes live without your sign-off.

What if I don't like a clip or quote it selected?

Skip it, replace it, or request alternatives. You're always in control.

Does it work for any preaching style?

Yes — whether you're expository, topical, narrative, or somewhere in between. The AI adapts to your style.
